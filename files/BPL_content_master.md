### Product 1: MiX Vision
**Category:** Video Telematics
> MiX Vision captures live footage using in-vehicle (both in-cab and forward-facing) and optional external cameras. This solution continuously captures rolling 72 hours of video for retrieval for an accurate view of what occurs before incidents to add context where it's needed most.

### Product 2: MiX Rovi II
**Category:** In-Cab Display

CTA: Buy Now → /contact

### Product 3: Wireless Axle Load Sensor (Air Suspension)
**Category:** Telematics Hardware
> Wireless pressure sensor GNOM DDE S7 is used in transport telematics systems (GPS/GLONASS vehicle monitoring systems) to monitor axle loads, determine the axle load in vehicles equipped with air suspension, prevent exceeding axle loads.
CTA: Buy Now → /contact

### Product 4: MiX Communicator Onboard Computer (MiX4000)
**Category:** Fleet Hardware
> On-board computer with GPS and GSM modem that enables the MiX Fleet Manager Premium solution.
CTA: Buy Now → /contact

### Product 5: Fuel Monitoring System
**Category:** Fuel Management
> Our system includes robust fuel management capabilities that allow you to monitor fuel consumption accurately. In addition, it provides tools to detect anomalies such as fuel theft or inefficient driving practices that may be leading to excessive fuel usage. By identifying and addressing these issues proactively, you can significantly reduce fuel costs and enhance operational efficiency.
CTA: Buy Now → /contact

### Product 6: Tamper-Resistant Speed Limiter
**Category:** Safety Hardware
> Our solution utilizes the Tamper-Resistant speed-limiting device to control your vehicle's maximum speed, enhancing safety, fuel efficiency, and regulatory compliance. The key features include an electronic control unit, speed sensors, adjustable speed settings, tamper-proof design, automatic activation, and data logging. The encoded electronic and adjustable actuator with lever system takes care of the desired maximum speed.
CTA: Buy Now → /contact

**Category:** Safety Hardware

## 11. COMPETITOR REFERENCE — MiX BY POWERFLEET (mixtelematics.com/us)

### What they do well (to beat):
- Clean dark/light split design
- Strong solution-by-outcome structure (Safety / Efficiency / Compliance / Security / Sustainability)
- Stats from credible third parties (Frost & Sullivan — 80% distraction reduction, 60% collision reduction, etc.)
- Video telematics emphasis with AI dashcams
- Global credibility: 120+ countries, 1.8M+ connections, 30 years experience, 24/7 support
- ABI Research #1 ranking badge
- Industry verticals landing pages (Oil & Gas, Mining, Transport & Logistics, etc.)

### What BPL should do differently (differentiation):
- **Nigeria-first positioning** — no Western competitor owns the Nigerian logistics narrative
- **Real-time dashboard product** (the CNL Chevron work) — position this as a live offering
- **Human story** — BPL has been here since 2001, trained 5,000+ Nigerian drivers
- **Interactive, alive UI** — MiX site is good but static
- **Local client proof** — Shell Nigeria, TotalEnergies, Lafarge Africa, DHL Nigeria are more relatable to Nigerian prospects than Frisch & Sons (USA)
