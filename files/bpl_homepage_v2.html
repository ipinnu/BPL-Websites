Our Story
2001

Two decades shaping Nigerian fleet safety

Founded in 2001, Best Practices Limited became Nigeria's strategic partner for MiX Telematics South Africa and Elson Truck Technology — deploying GPS tracking, road speed limiters, and fleet management SaaS across the country's most demanding enterprise fleets.

Today we serve oil & gas, FMCG, construction, and transport — with Shell, TotalEnergies, DHL, and Lafarge among our valued clients.
